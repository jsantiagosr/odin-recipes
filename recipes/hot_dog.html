Classic Hot Dog (VERY HOT)

Ingredients

  • SPICY SAUSAGE
  • Bread
  • Ketchup
  • Romanian Onion

Steps to follow to make it

  1. Buy the bread, sausage, and ketchup
  2. Cook the sausage a little bit
  3. Heat the bread
  4. Put all together while thinking about how a hot dog should should look like
  5. Add the final touch with the Onion
